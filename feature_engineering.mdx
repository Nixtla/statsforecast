Decompose the series into trend and seasonal using the MSTL model. 



**Args:**
 
 - <b>`df`</b> (pandas or polars DataFrame):  DataFrame with columns [`unique_id`, `ds`, `y`]. 
 - <b>`model`</b> (statsforecast MSTL):  Model to use for the decomposition. 
 - <b>`freq`</b> (str):  Frequency of the data (pandas alias). 
 - <b>`h`</b> (int):  Forecast horizon. 



**Returns:**
 
 - <b>`Tuple[DataFrame, DataFrame]`</b>:  A tuple containing: 
        - train_df (pandas or polars DataFrame): Original dataframe with the 'trend' and 'seasonal' columns added. 
        - X_df (pandas or polars DataFrame): Future dataframe to be provided to the predict method through `X_df`.
